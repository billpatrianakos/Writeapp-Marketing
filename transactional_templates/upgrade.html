Need to save a thought quick? Or maybe you just prefer writing in your email client. Now you can save notes just by sending an email! 2-Factor Authentication For extra security.

Make sure your private notes stay private by adding an extra layer of security to your account. With 2-factor authentication enabled, if you ever lose access to your account or a bad guy tries to reset your password we'll not only send you a recovery email but a text message with a unique PIN as well. Now the bad guys will need your email password and your cell phone to gain access to your account. (Plus we'll be adding 2-factor auth everywhere soon).
